HOW AN ENGAGEMENT OPENS

A documented onboarding ledger — not a guessing game.

Every new firm partnership runs through the same five-entry process, so you know exactly what to expect and when your first deliverable lands.

ENTRY 001

Discovery & Scope Call

We map your current client load, software stack, SLAs and review process in a single 30-minute call — no generic questionnaire.

Output: a written scope and capacity plan within 24 hours.

ENTRY 002

NDA & Data Security Setup

Mutual NDA, access provisioning via your preferred secure channel, and role-based permissions — we never request more access than the task requires.

Output: signed agreements and a locked-down access matrix.

ENTRY 003

Team Matching & Training

We assign a named CA (or team) matched to your client mix and train them on your specific chart of accounts, templates and review checkpoints.

Output: a dedicated team roster with direct contact lines.

ENTRY 004

Parallel-Run Month

Your first month runs in parallel with your existing process — you review every output against your own benchmark before we take full ownership.

Output: a side-by-side quality report and sign-off.

ENTRY 005

Full Handover & Monthly Cadence

We take full ownership of the agreed scope on a fixed monthly close calendar, with a standing review call and an open escalation line.

Output: a recurring close calendar your firm can plan around.

QUESTIONS FIRMS ASK BEFORE THEY SIGN

Straight answers, before you commit.

Will our clients know the work is outsourced?+

No. We operate entirely under your firm's branding, templates, and email domain. Deliverables, communication style, and file formats are matched to your existing client-facing standards.

Who actually does the work — is it really Chartered Accountants?+

Yes. Every engagement is led by a qualified Chartered Accountant, with senior review built into our internal process before anything reaches your team. We don't staff client files with untrained data-entry operators.

How is our data and our clients' data protected?+

Mutual NDAs are signed before access is granted, access is role-based and limited to the assigned scope, and our workstations and network practices follow SOC-2 aligned controls. We never request broader access than a task requires.

What if we want to scale up quickly during tax season?+

Our seasonal surge model is built for exactly this — most firms can add capacity within 5–7 business days without a long-term retainer commitment.

How is pricing structured?+

We offer per-client pricing, a dedicated monthly staff-seat model, and project-based seasonal pricing. Every quote is scoped to your client complexity and volume on the discovery call — no generic rate cards.

What happens during the first month?+

Your first month runs as a parallel test against your existing process or benchmark, so you can verify quality before we take full ownership of the books.
